# Support Paladin Build – Season 13 (Lord of Hatred)

---

## Overview

The Support Paladin is the premier group support build in Season 13. It revolves around the Paladin's **Aura system**, introduced with the Lord of Hatred expansion, providing the group with attack speed, crit chance, resistances, healing, and enemy grouping — all in one package.

> ⚠️ **Range caveat:** Aura range is relatively small. You and your DPS must stay close together at all times. Fanaticism Aura does not update properly if players run in and out of range.

---

## Role in Group

| Contribution | Source |
|---|---|
| Crit Strike Chance + Attack Speed | Fanaticism Aura |
| Armor + All Resistances + Unstoppable | Defiance Aura |
| Healing + Fortify | Holy Light Aura |
| Resolve stacks (×5/sec) | Rite of Prayer |
| Fortify to allies | Rite of Mercy |
| Thorns + Thorns damage buff | Rite of Thorns |
| Enemy grouping + Stun | Condemn + Kry rune |
| Vulnerable on enemies | Gather the Guilty |

---

## Core Skills

- **Fanaticism Aura** – Crit Strike Chance and Attack Speed per stack (scales with Aura Potency and ranks)
- **Defiance Aura** – Armor/All Resistances buff; Unstoppable on manual cast
- **Holy Light Aura** – Healing + Fortify for group
- **Condemn** – Groups enemies, stuns, applies Vulnerable via Gather the Guilty
- **Fortress** – Large protective circle; cast on top of grouped enemies
- **Purify** – Cast off cooldown

---

## Key Gear

| Item | Why |
|---|---|
| **Iron Conviction Set** (Charm/Talisman) | 3-pc: Unlocks all Aura upgrades for free. 5-pc: Conviction Aura — enemies take more damage per Aura Skill Point |
| **Sundered Night** | Casts Consecration on any Aura Skill use; +200% Aura Potency while standing in Consecration |
| **Harlequin Crest** | Massive defensives + 4 ranks to all skills |
| **Sanctis of Kethamar** (Charm) | +30% Aura Potency |
| **Temerity** | Barrier for extra defense |
| **Cir + Vex** (Runeword) | +3 ranks to all Skills |
| **Neo + Kry** (Runeword) | Non-stop enemy pull for grouping |

---

## Gameplay Loop

1. Group enemies with **Condemn** + Kry pull
2. Cast **Fortress** on top of grouped enemies
3. Cast **Fanaticism Aura**, **Defiance Aura**, **Holy Light Aura** off cooldown → places Consecration under you (via Sundered Night)
4. Cast **Purify** off cooldown
5. Stay within Aura range of your group at all times

---

## Season 13 Context

- Paladin is a **new permanent class** added with Lord of Hatred expansion
- Support Paladin uses the **Iron Conviction Set** — one of the first class Set Charms in D4's history
- Paladin was slightly nerfed at S13 launch but remains extremely strong
- This is the **only dedicated group support build** in the current meta
- Talisman system allows build-defining Uniques to be converted to Charms, freeing up gear slots

---

## Pros & Cons

**Pros:**
- True group support — brings buffs no other class can replicate
- Strong survivability when geared correctly
- Very impactful in group endgame (Pit, War Plans, Infernal Hordes)
- Satisfying "hold hands" team playstyle

**Cons:**
- Small Aura range — group must stay close
- Fanaticism Aura has a known range-update bug (players moving in/out lose buff temporarily)
- Requires LoH expansion
- Less impactful when playing solo
