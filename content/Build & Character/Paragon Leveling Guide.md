# Paragon Leveling Guide – Season 13 (Lord of Hatred)

---

## Overview

In Season 13, the Paragon cap has been significantly expanded alongside the new level cap of 70 (up from 60). Glyph progression is also deeper — glyphs now level to **150** (up from 100), and the threshold to turn a Glyph Legendary has moved from level 25 to **50**. Paragon Points are also awarded through the **Season Rank** track, making seasonal objectives worth completing.

> ⚠️ Raw stat additive damage nodes have been largely removed from Paragon boards. Boards now exist to serve **Glyph bonuses**, **Legendary nodes**, and **skill-tag synergies**.

| Source | Notes |

## 🧍 Solo – Best Activities for Paragon XP

### 1. The Pit (Primary Loop)
- The central endgame activity in S13 — not optional.
- Push Pit tiers as high as you can sustain efficiently.
- Higher tiers = faster mob density clears = faster XP.
- Efficient Pit farming beats most other activities for raw XP/hour solo.
- Glyph XP is also earned here — critical for Paragon power.

### 2. Nightmare Dungeons (NMDs)
- Still solid for XP, especially in the 60–80 NMD tier range.
- Now integrated into **War Plans** – use War Plans to queue NMDs with customized modifiers (extra monster density, loot focus, boss spawns mid-run).
- Pick affixes that don't waste time (avoid ones that punish movement).

### 3. Echoing Hatred
- Triggered via rare **Trace of Echoes** item.
- Endless wave survival mode — excellent XP density if your build handles it.
- Builds typically hit a wall at wave 20–30 if not optimized.
- Best used when you have a strong build and want to stress-test it while gaining XP.

### 4. Helltide
- Consistent background XP — always running somewhere on the map.
- Good for multi-tasking: farm Forgotten Souls, Whispers objectives, AND XP simultaneously.
- Use War Plans to chain Helltide efficiently.

### 5. Season Rank Objectives
- Complete Season Rank challenges actively — they award Paragon Points directly.
- 9 ranks, 100+ total objectives. Knock these out naturally alongside other farming.

## 👥 Team / Group – Best Activities for Paragon XP

### 1. The Pit (Group Push)
- Group Pit runs are faster than solo at equivalent tiers.
- One player focuses on elites/bosses, others handle density clear.
- Higher tiers become accessible in group, meaning better XP scaling.
- Glyph XP still earned at same rate — everyone benefits.

### 2. Helltide (4-man Group)
- Mob density and event scaling increase with group size.
- Events like Accursed Rituals are faster and more rewarding.
- Rotate between events while others finish previous ones — near-zero downtime.

### 3. Nightmare Dungeons (via War Plans – Coordinated)
- One player coordinates War Plans playlist; group runs optimized NMD builds.
- Works best if group has at least one AoE/mob-clear build and one single-target.
- Use modifiers that spawn bonus monsters or accelerate timers.

### 4. Infernal Hordes (Group)
- Wave-based content benefits significantly from group DPS and crowd control.
- More Infernal Compasses needed — coordinate with group on who brings them.
- Scales well with group composition (tank + DPS + buffer setup).

### 5. Lair Bosses (Rotational)
- Less about raw XP, more about loot efficiency — use to unlock better gear faster, which accelerates Pit progress.
- In group: rotate boss kills for faster materials and avoid lockout downtime.

## ⚡ Efficiency Tips

- **Prioritize Glyph XP alongside Paragon XP** — Glyphs are now the primary power driver on boards. A level 50 Glyph (Legendary threshold) can define your entire build.
- **Use War Plans** to create a consistent activity rotation. Don't aimlessly wander — War Plans let you customize modifiers and chain activities efficiently.
- **Smoldering Ashes – Urn of Glyphs**: activate this Season Blessing for a chance at bonus Glyph upgrades per run — synergizes directly with Paragon power.
- **Don't skip Season Rank objectives** — the Paragon Point rewards are free power.
- **Warlock / Paladin players**: both new classes have high Paragon synergy but require more deliberate board pathing — map out skill-tag synergies before investing heavily.
- **Skill Variants matter**: if you switch a skill's elemental tag via a Skill Variant, verify your Paragon board still synergizes — tag changes cascade into board interactions.

## 🗺️ Recommended Activity Priority (General)

```
Early Endgame (fresh 70):
  Season Rank Objectives → Helltide → NMDs (War Plans) → Pit (low tiers)

Mid Endgame (Torment 4–8):
  Pit farming → Lair Bosses (for gear) → Infernal Hordes → Echoing Hatred

Late Endgame (Torment 9–12):
  Pit pushing (solo or group) → Echoing Hatred deep waves → War Plans optimized loops
```
