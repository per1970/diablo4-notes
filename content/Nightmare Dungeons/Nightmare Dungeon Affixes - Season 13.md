# Nightmare Dungeon Affixes — Season 13

> Each Nightmare Sigil rolls with **exactly one positive affix** and **2–4 negative (enemy/monster) affixes** depending on tier. Positive affixes are always listed first on the sigil tooltip.

---

## ✅ Positive Affixes — Ranked Best → Worst

| Rank | Affix | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Horadric Strongroom** | Guarantees a Horadric Strongroom in the dungeon — extra Obducite, loot, and bonus buffs |
| 2 | **Chaos Rifts** | Guarantees Chaos Rifts within the dungeon — bonus combat encounters with strong rewards |
| 3 | **Equipment Delve** | Spawns collectible items that upgrade a cache of gear (Weapons, Jewelry, Armor) |
| 4 | **Treasure Breach** | Gilded Gremlins invade — kill them for bonus loot drops |
| 5 | **Horadric Reserves** | Spawns loot chests with Gem Fragments, Gold, Obols, Seasonal Currency, and Resources |
| 6 | **Forgotten Wisdom** | XP spawns on kill + an XP well spawns in the dungeon — best pure XP affix |
| 7 | **Ancestral Awakening** | +10% Experience — simple and always useful |
| 8 | **Power Shrines** | Additional shrines spawn (Artillery, Blast Wave, Channeling, Lethal, Protection) — situational |
| 9 | **Gold Find** | +30% Gold — least impactful in endgame |

> [!tip] **Best-case sigil:** Horadric Strongroom or Chaos Rifts paired with manageable negatives = ideal run. Never salvage a Strongroom sigil unless the negatives are truly build-breaking.

## ❌ Enemy / Negative Affixes — Ranked Worst → Most Manageable

| Rank | Affix | Effect | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Stormbane's Wrath** | Chases players, releases deadly pulses on contact | Most lethal affix; punishes slow/stationary builds; avoid on Hardcore |
| 2 | **Empowered Elites (Suppressor)** | Elites always have the Suppressor bubble | Completely counters ranged builds; forces melee to pop the bubble |
| 3 | **Drifting Shade** | Chases player, explodes on contact, creates a Dazing Nightmare Field | Very dangerous on Hardcore; avoid if you have low movement |
| 4 | **Nightmare Portal** | Portals spawn mid-combat and pour out dangerous enemies | Overwhelms low-clear-speed builds; manageable with AoE |
| 5 | **Empowered Elites (Shock Lance)** | Elites always have Electric Lance | Devastating for builds sensitive to interruption or those with no Lightning Res |
| 6 | **Empowered Elites (Poison Enchanted)** | Elites always apply Poison | Requires potion or healing to purge; stack Poison Resistance to soften |
| 7 | **Monster Barrier** | Monsters gain 85% of max life as a Barrier | Significantly inflates effective enemy HP; slows clear speed noticeably |
| 8 | **Elemental Totems** | Totems spawn and buff nearby enemies until destroyed | Disruptive but destroyable; prioritize the totem first |
| 9 | **Blood Blister** | Killing monsters may spawn an exploding Blood Blister | Punishes fast pack clears; watch your feet after big kills |
| 10 | **Avenger** | Killing a monster enrages nearby monsters (more damage) | Snowballs in dense pulls; be aware during large AoE clears |
| 11 | **Volcanic** | Periodic flame eruptions near players | Lowest-threat negative; easily dodged by any mobile build |

> [!warning] **Affix impact is build-dependent.** Slow builds hate movement-punishing affixes (Stormbane, Drifting Shade). Barrier builds hate Monster Barrier. Ranged builds especially hate Suppressor. Always evaluate against your specific playstyle.

> [!note] **Escalating Nightmares stack affixes.** The 3-dungeon Escalation chain inherits affixes from each previous dungeon, ending with 3 positive + 3 negative affixes in the final dungeon before the Astaroth boss fight.
