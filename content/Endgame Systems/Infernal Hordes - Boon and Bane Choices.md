# Infernal Hordes — Boon and Bane Choices

## The Golden Rule

**Hellborne > Elites > everything else.**

Hellborne are the highest-Aether enemies in the activity. Stacking Boons that **spawn more of them, empower them, and increase their Aether drops** snowballs your run harder than any other strategy.

> [!tip] Combo to chase
> Combining "Surging Hellborn", "Exalted Hellborn", "Surging Elites" and "Unstoppable Elites" gives you explosive Aether if they appear in time.

## S-Tier Picks — Always Take

These four Hellborne offerings all stack:

| Offering | Boon / Bane |
|---|---|
| **The Burning Hellborne** | Hellfire spawns Hellborne / Hellborne drop +2 Aether |
| **The Summoned Hellborne** | Hellborne hunt you / Hellborne grant +1 Aether |
| **The Exalted Hellborne** | Hellborne damage +25% / Hellborne grant +1 Aether |
| **The Surging Hellborne** | +1 extra Hellborne when spawned / Hellborne grant +1 Aether |

> [!star] Best single pick
> **The Burning Hellborne** is the strongest individual pick — it generates *more* Hellborne via Hellfire and gives the biggest individual Aether bump.

## A-Tier — Strong Filler

When Hellborne aren't on offer, take these next:

- **The Exalted Hordes** — normal monster damage +25%, killing them spawns Aether Events 50% faster (excellent for AoE clear builds)
- **The Surging Elite** — Elite spawn chance doubled, Aether Fiends grant +2
- **The Exalted Elite** — Elite damage +25%, Aether Fiends grant +2
- **The Unstoppable Elite** — Elites become Unstoppable (you can't CC them), Aether Fiends grant +2
- **The Burning Rain** — Hellfire rains on you, but spawns 3-9 Aether at end of each wave
  - **Big synergy with The Burning Hellborne** (more Hellfire = more Hellborne)

## ALWAYS Grab Chaos Waves

This is the key thing many players still miss.

**Chaos Waves are a 4th additional choice on top of the three Infernal Offers.** They significantly change the battlefield by introducing unique mechanics and a much higher level of difficulty. In exchange, they reward you with **substantial Burning Aether** if you can overcome them.

> [!warning] Pick them every time
> Whenever possible, always pick Chaos Waves — the Aether rewards far exceed regular waves.

The number you'll see depends on your **Compass Tier** — higher tier compasses = more Chaos Wave opportunities. Skip only if your build is genuinely too fragile to handle the modifiers stacking.

## B-Tier — Situational

- **Council-related offerings** (Exalted Council, Enfeebled/Exhausted/Withered Wanderer) — only pay 15 Aether *at the end* during the Fell Council fight
  - Worth taking late if your Aether total is just shy of a chest threshold (e.g., at 380 needing 400 for Greater Equipment)
  - Skippable otherwise
- **The Invigorating Hellborne** — Hellborne damage +25% but they Invigorate you on kill
  - Promotes to A-tier if you're struggling with sustain

## Avoid (C/D Tier)

Anything **Aetheric Mass, Soulspire-focused, or Aether Lord-focused**. They sound flavorful but the Aether-per-time math is much worse than Hellborne/Elite stacking.

Some boons are less effective, like the ones that add spires giving only minor Aether increments. Others speed up normal monster spawns which helps with more events but gives less Aether per kill.

## Spending Aether at the End

After clearing the Fell Council (or paying 666 Aether to fight Bartuc instead):

| Chest | Cost | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| **Spoils of Greater Equipment** | 400 Aether | Guaranteed Ancestral Legendary with a Greater Affix ⭐ |
| **Spoils of Materials** | 20 Aether per open | Masterworking mats and Scrolls of Restoration |
| **Spoils of Gold** | Variable | Gold (use leftovers) |

> [!note] Open as many as you can afford
> You can open chests as many times as you have Aether for. A great run might net you 2-3 Greater Equipment chests in one shot.

## Group Play Tip

In a group, **all party members earn Burning Aether for themselves** and decide how to spend it at the end of the run.

- Each player has their own Aether pool
- Each player chooses their own chests
- Boon/Bane choices are **shared per wave** — coordinate with whoever's clicking
- Make sure to have your pet accompany you — they pick up Aether for the host only
- **Each player needs their own pet active** to maximize pickup

## Run Priority Cheat Sheet

1. **Always pick Chaos Waves** when offered (4th option)
2. **Always stack Hellborne** offerings if available
3. **Burning Hellborne + Burning Rain** is the strongest synergy combo
4. **Elites** are your fallback when Hellborne aren't available
5. Skip Mass/Spire/Lord offerings unless nothing better is on the table
6. Aim for **400+ Aether** to crack at least one Spoils of Greater Equipment
