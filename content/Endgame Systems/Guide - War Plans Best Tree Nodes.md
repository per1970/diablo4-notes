# Guide – War Plans: Best Tree Nodes

> Source: wudijo (YouTube, May 1, 2026) + Mobalytics / Keengamer / Vortex Gaming cross-reference
> ⚠️ Early-season assessment — meta still being mapped. Treat recommendations as starting points.

---

## System Overview

War Plans is a **permanent endgame feature** (not seasonal) unlocked after completing the Lord of Hatred campaign. Access it via the **Command Table in Temis** (Skovos). Requires **Torment difficulty** to activate.

- Build a custom playlist of up to **5 endgame activities**, complete them in sequence, collect a **War Chest** at the end
- Each activity has its own **Activity Skill Tree** — spend points to customize modifiers, rewards, and spawns for that activity
- XP earned toward a tree happens whenever you complete that activity, even outside an active War Plan; however, **node unlocks only trigger during active War Plan runs**
- **Lair Boss tree** requires Torment 1+; **Pit tree** requires completing Season Rank 2 (Hellish Descent capstone)

### ⚠️ Respec Warning
Respec costs scale steeply at higher ranks — reportedly **~46–50 million gold per point** at upper levels. Plan your pathing before you reach that tier. Early ranks are cheap to experiment with; later ranks are not.

### General Philosophy
- Depth beats breadth: a fully specced tree for one activity outperforms three half-built trees
- Prioritize the activities you enjoy or need the most (boss loot tables, XP, Forgotten Souls, Glyphs)
- XP is a persistent bottleneck in S13 — Paragon points are valuable, leveling is slow. Invest in XP nodes whenever they appear at low opportunity cost

## Tree-by-Tree Breakdown

### 🌳 Tree of Whispers
**Tier: C — Low priority, do for incidental rewards**

Runs passively alongside your active War Plan. Not a primary farming target.

- **Spin and Spark / Material Cache path**: Gives Forgotten Souls, gold, crafting mats. Useful at higher Torment when you no longer need items but want materials.
- **Mystery Cache path** (untested by wudijo): Potential Spark income — worth investigating as Mythics are scarce early in the season.
- Corrupted Roots / head rotten guys nodes: Not yet assessed.

*Recommendation: Invest 3 points in the middle path for bonus XP on Whisper cache turn-ins. Use leftover points for material or gear caches based on your current needs.*

---

### ⚔️ Nightmare Dungeons
**Tier: B — Good if you're farming Astaroth or want Goblin loot**

- **Middle Path (Escalation Sigils / Recurring Nightmare)**: Core node unlocks infinite Escalating Nightmare Sigils, giving near-endless access to the **Astaroth** boss loot table. Essential if your build wants a specific Astaroth ring or Unique.
- **Left Path (Shrine + Revive mechanic)**: Monsters revive and can be carried — stacks with Nemesis for extra enemies and loot. Nice but not exceptional.
- **Right Path (Goblins + Treasure Breaches)** *(recommended per Mobalytics)*: Treasure Goblins drop high-quality loot, mats, and have Mythic Unique potential. If self-sustaining Treasure Breach rotations are confirmed viable, this path could be very strong.
- **Tuned Primordial Dust** (Left path): Only source of this material on the tree. Needed to reroll Unique affix values. Niche but potentially important for Unique-heavy builds.

*Recommendation: Middle path if you need Astaroth. Right path (Goblins) for general loot hunting.*

---

### 🔥 Helltide
**Tier: S — Best overall activity; top XP and material source**

- **Middle Path — Maggots / Hellwyrm node** ⭐: The standout pick. Every Hellwyrm spawn produces Maggots that drop massive experience orbs. At T12, a single Hellwyrm event can yield **20–30 million XP**. Also spawns a **Pang of Duriel** that drops Lair Keys — making this the best combined source of XP + Lair Keys + Boss Trophies in the game.
  - Boss Trophies can be turned in at the Horadric Cube for Uniques.
  - Middle path's deeper nodes are weaker — generally skip them.
- **Left Path (Profane Mindcages)**: Increases monster level in Helltide for more cinders, XP, and drops at the cost of difficulty.
- **Hellborn Butchers / 4 additional Hellborn**: Strong if your build can handle the extra elites. Good use of leftover points.
- Right side (rats, extra cinders, big chest): Untested — may be a cinder dump mechanism rather than a meaningful upgrade.

*Recommendation: Middle path (Maggots/Hellwyrm) first — best ROI in the game. Then left path or Hellborn nodes with remaining points.*

---

### 🏛️ Undercity
**Tier: B — Excellent Forgotten Souls farm; efficient tribute usage**

- **Left Path — Invasion Portals + Portal Pranksters** ⭐: Invasion portals spawn during runs and drop extra rewards including **Forgotten Souls** — the most in-demand crafting material in S13. Portal Pranksters node compounds this.
- **Leftover Tribute dump node**: Lets you open all remaining small tribute chests at end of run without running another Undercity. Significant time saver.
- XP node (1 point): Low investment, applies to all characters. Worth picking up.

*Recommendation: Left path down to Portal Pranksters + Tribute dump. Best consistent Forgotten Souls source.*

---

### 👑 Lair Bosses
**Tier: A — Best targeted loot; Nemesis chain is potentially the best Mythic source**

- **Boss targeting nodes**: Invest in the specific boss whose loot table you need. Check Maxroll once tables are fully mapped.
  - Example: Infernal Hordes farming → add free Varshan via cross-tree node
- **Middle Path — Nemesis Lair** ⭐: After killing a boss and opening the loot chest, triggers a **dual boss fight**, which can chain into a **triple boss fight**. Community reports suggest this is one of the best Mythic drop sources in the game. Requires a strong build; dropping a Torment tier is a valid workaround.
- Requires Torment 1+ to unlock; Greater Lair Boss nodes require Torment 6+.

*Recommendation: Middle path (Nemesis chain) for Mythic farming once your build is capable. Otherwise invest in your priority boss loot table first.*

---

### 🌊 Infernal Hordes
**Tier: B — Flexible loot targeting with the right node**

- **Method in Madness** ⭐: Allows consistent targeting of a specific Infernal offering strategy — Soulspire, Lord synergies, or Masses — giving near-guaranteed repetition of your preferred offering type.
  - Trade-off: speccing into this blocks Chaos Waves. Keep Chaos Waves open if you want cross-boss drops (e.g. Varshan) to proc there.
  - Hybrid option: don't block Chaos Waves so you can pivot between strategies as needed.
- Right side of tree: currently assessed as weak — not recommended.
- Deeper nodes: still being tested; potentially strong but unverified.

*Recommendation: Method in Madness first. Decide whether to block Chaos Waves based on whether you want cross-boss drops.*

---

### ⚒️ The Pit
**Tier: B — Core glyph leveling loop; XP is secondary**

The Pit has been nerfed compared to prior seasons. Its primary value in S13 is **glyph XP**, not loot.

- **Chrono's Blessing (Children's Haste)** ⭐: +2 glyph upgrades per run when completed sub-5 minutes. First priority — directly doubles glyph XP efficiency on fast runs.
- **Heart of Stone mechanic**: Spawns at run start — pick it up and don't drop it. Surviving with it to the end gives +1 glyph upgrade and significantly more progression orbs, shaving ~20–30 seconds off run time.
- **Middle Path — Progression Orb XP node**: Boosts XP from all progression orbs including Heart of Stone orbs. Adds ~10 million XP as a free bonus at run end.
- **XP summary**: Fast runs (2–3 min) yield ~5M+ XP in-run + ~10M from orb dump = ~15M XP per run. Consistent but lower peak than a Hellwyrm event.
- **Right side (Chaon's Wager, Artificer Stones, Cursed Rituals, Cursed Offerings)**: wudijo's verdict — currently trash. Events are too rare and too easy to miss mid-run. May improve with more data.

*Recommendation: Chrono's Blessing → Heart of Stone node → Middle XP path. Skip right side for now.*

## XP Farming Summary

XP is slow relative to gear and glyph progression in S13. Paragon points are more valuable than in previous seasons. Invest XP nodes liberally across trees when the opportunity cost is low.

| Source | XP per Event | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Hellwyrm Maggots (T12) | ~20–30M burst | Best single XP event in game; occasional |
| Pit run (fast build) | ~5M in-run + ~10M orb dump | Most consistent; every 2–3 min |
| Undercity XP node | Moderate | Applies to all characters |

## Quick Priority Checklist

- [ ] Helltide → Middle path (Maggots/Hellwyrm) first
- [ ] Undercity → Left path (Portals + Tribute dump) for Forgotten Souls
- [ ] Pit → Chrono's Blessing + Heart of Stone + middle XP path
- [ ] Lair Bosses → Nemesis chain (middle) once build is capable
- [ ] Nightmare Dungeons → Middle (Astaroth sigils) or Right (Goblins) based on goal
- [ ] Infernal Hordes → Method in Madness
- [ ] Tree of Whispers → Low priority; mid path XP or mat cache with spare points
