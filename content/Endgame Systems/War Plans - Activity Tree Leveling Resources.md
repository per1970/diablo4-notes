# War Plans — Activity Tree Leveling Resources

## Top Pick: Maxroll

**[War Plans — D4 Maxroll.gg](https://maxroll.gg/d4/resources/war-plans)**

Maxroll is a top-tier Diablo resource (alongside Icy Veins). The page is **actively maintained** with current S13 skill tree and node data.

### Why it's the best

- Explicit coverage of **Activity Experience scaling**: 25 XP per activity in Normal up to 118 XP in Torment 12
- Explains why the **Whispers tree levels faster** than the others (no Whisper War Plan nodes — XP comes from completing Whispers in the world)
- Each skill tree caps at **7 levels**, gaining 1 skill point per level
- Concrete node examples most other guides skip:
  - Entering **Nemesis dungeons** after defeating Lair Bosses (multiple Lair Bosses at once)
  - **Bonus loot showers** after killing Infernal Council/Bartuc in Infernal Hordes
  - **Hellwyrns in Helltides** spawning worms that drop tons of XP and Aberrant Cinders
  - Chance for **Varshan to appear** when completing a Whisper (drops Ancestral Collection of Chaos contents)
  - **+4 extra Glyph upgrade chances** in The Pit (total 9 per run)

## Backup Reads

### KeenGamer

**[Diablo IV Lord of Hatred – War Plans Guide](https://www.keengamer.com/articles/guides/diablo-iv-lord-of-hatred-war-plans-guide/)**

Strongest piece on **Season 13 Season Journey integration**. Lists specific Season Rank milestones requiring Activity Levels:

| Rank | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Rank III | Activity Level 2 on any 2 War Plan Activities |
| Rank V | Activity Level 3 on any 3 War Plan Activities |
| Rank VII | Activity Level 4 on any 4 War Plan Activities |
| Rank IX | Activity Level 5 on any 5 War Plan Activities |

Useful for planning which trees to push and in what order to maximize Season Rank rewards (free Paragon Points and Skill Points).

### AOEAH

**[Diablo 4 War Plans Skill Tree Builds, Rewards & Farming Strats](https://www.aoeah.com/news/4501--diablo-4-war-plans-skill-tree-builds-rewards--farming-strats)**

Slightly older (3 weeks) but goes deeper on **strategic philosophy**:

- "Specialize, don't spread" argument
- Rerolling-resets-rank tradeoff
- Why some nodes (like the deathless cinder modifier) are only worth taking if your build can support them

## Recommended Reading Order

1. **Maxroll first** — mechanical foundation (tree functions, XP scaling per Torment, full node lists)
2. **KeenGamer second** — align tree investment with Season Journey milestones
3. **AOEAH third** — meta-strategy for spending points once you understand the basics

## Universal S13 Advice

All three sources converge on: **specialize early**.

> [!tip] Don't spread thin
> With 7 points per tree and more than double that in available nodes, spreading across every tree produces weak bonuses everywhere. Pick **1 or 2 activities** and invest deeply before branching out.
