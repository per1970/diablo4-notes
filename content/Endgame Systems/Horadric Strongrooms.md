# Horadric Strongrooms — Season 13

> Horadric Strongrooms are **optional timed challenge rooms found inside Nightmare Dungeons**. Short, fast-paced, densely packed with enemies — and absolutely worth running if your goal is loot and Obducite.

---

## How to Find a Strongroom

Strongrooms do not appear in every Nightmare Dungeon. Ways to increase your chances:

- **Play on higher World Tiers** — Torment difficulty increases the chance of a Strongroom spawning.
- **Use a Nightmare Sigil with the Horadric Strongroom affix** — this *guarantees* a Strongroom in that dungeon. Never salvage these sigils unless the negatives are truly build-killing.
- **Watch for a flash of light and a distinct sound** inside the dungeon — that signals a Horadric Gateway is nearby.
- **Follow the purple wisps** of energy from slain monsters; they are drawn toward the Gateway and will appear on the minimap when you're close.

Once found: **defeat the initial ambush** to open the portal and enter.

---

## Inside the Strongroom — Step by Step

### 1. Choose Horadric Statues (Pillars)

After completing the first ritual inside the Strongroom, you are presented with **5 random Statues** from the pool and must pick **up to 3**. Higher difficulty (Torment tiers) lets you activate more statues and reveals stronger options. Your choices are locked in for the entire run — there's no switching midway.

Statues fall into two categories:

#### ✅ Pure / Blessed Statues
Retain their original Horadric intent. They buff your character or the run without adding significant danger. Lower risk, lower ceiling.

#### ☠️ Corrupted Statues (Astaroth-Influenced)
Corrupted by Astaroth's influence. They make the run significantly harder — stronger enemies, environmental hazards, debuffs — but offer **greatly increased Attunement gain and reward potential**. These are the statues to take if your build can handle the pressure.

## Horadric Statue Types — Ranked Best → Worst

> ⚠️ **Important note:** Statues are drawn semi-randomly from a pool and the game provides poor tooltip clarity — a known community complaint. The specific 5 offered to you each run will vary. Not all statues below will appear in every run. Rankings reflect general community consensus on Attunement efficiency and build-agnostic value.

### 🏆 S-Tier — Take These Every Time

| Statue / Test Type | Category | Effect | Why It's Best |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Extra Mob Waves** | Pure or Corrupted | More enemies spawn from shrines and pillars throughout the run | More bodies = more Attunement. Best for AoE builds; universally strong |
| **Demonic Summoning** | Corrupted | Hit Attunement thresholds mid-run to summon elite enemies (including The Butcher) | High-risk, huge Attunement spike. Only take if your build can kill elites quickly |
| **Astral Pillar** | Corrupted | Launches additional waves at Executable (non-boss) enemies to Execute them | Clears packs fast, chains Attunement bursts. Strong with high-damage builds |
| **Cosmic Anomaly** | Corrupted | Implosion Executes non-boss enemies; successful Executions grant +20% Attack Speed for 8 sec | Free clear speed and attack speed stacking. Excellent with fast-attacking builds |

### ✅ A-Tier — Strong, Usually Worth Taking

| Statue / Test Type | Category | Effect | Why It's Good |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Enhanced Shrines** | Pure | Activated shrines grant additional effects — movement speed, AoE pulses, chain triggers | Amplifies Sacrifice Shrine value, which is already strong post-ritual |
| **Pillar Buffs** | Pure | Extends the 10-second Pillar kill buff window OR improves Attunement yield from circle-clears | Directly boosts your highest Attunement moments — stacks well |
| **Movement Speed Boost** | Pure | Grants major movement speed — can break the 200% movement cap | Speeds up the entire run rhythm, especially on cooldown-heavy builds |
| **Automated Ritual Progress** | Pure | Speeds up ritual mechanics, reducing time spent standing on pillar circles | Effectively gives you more free kill time during the 100-second window |

### ⚠️ B-Tier — Situational, Build-Dependent

| Statue / Test Type | Category | Effect | When to Take It |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Strange Afflictions** | Corrupted | Environmental hazards, curse effects, or enemy modifiers applied to the run | Higher risk, moderate reward bump — take only if hazards don't counter your build |
| **Passive Attunement Gain** | Pure | Fills the Attunement bar incrementally without requiring kills near pillars | Useful for builds with poor AoE or single-target clears; otherwise redundant |
| **Cooldown Reduction** | Pure | Reduces skill cooldowns during the run | Strong for CD-gated burst builds; minimal impact on spammable skill builds |
| **Damage Bonus** | Pure | Flat damage increase during the Strongroom | Helpful, but only if it meaningfully speeds up your kill rate to stack more Attunement |

### ❌ C / Avoid — Low Value or Actively Harmful

| Statue / Test Type | Category | Effect | Why to Avoid |
|---|---|---|---|
| **No-Potion Restriction** | Corrupted | Disables healing potions during the run in exchange for higher rewards | A death spiral affix for builds without built-in healing. Almost never worth it |
| **No-Evade Restriction** | Corrupted | Disables Evade during the run | Crippling for Evade-reliant builds (e.g. Evade Spiritborn). Skip entirely on those builds |
| **Vague/Unclear Modifiers** | Any | Effects that require interacting with specific ground items or triggers without clear UI guidance | Poor in-game clarity means players often miss the mechanic entirely, losing value |
| **Enemy Resistance Buffs** | Corrupted | Enemies gain resistance to your primary damage type | Directly counters your damage output — avoid unless you have multiple damage types |

### General Statue Selection Strategy

- **AoE / screen-clear builds** (Ball Lightning Sorc, Whirlwind Barb, Warlock swarm): Prioritize **Extra Mob Waves, Enhanced Shrines, Demonic Summoning** — more bodies = more Attunement.
- **Single-target / precision builds** (Poison Rogue, melee Paladin): Prioritize **Pillar Buffs, Cooldown Reduction, Passive Attunement Gain** — reward precision clears over mass spawns.
- **Any build on Hardcore**: Avoid **No-Potion, No-Evade, and Demonic Summoning** unless your survivability is extremely high.
- **Always pick Corrupted statues if your build can handle them** — the Attunement and reward ceiling is substantially higher than Pure options.

## Race the Clock — Earn Horadric Attunement

- You have **100 seconds** to fill the Attunement bar.
- **Every kill grants Attunement.** Your score is displayed top-right, next to the minimap.
- **Deaths incur a penalty** (same as the Pit) — stay alive.
- Completing a **Pillar Ritual** triggers a **10-second window** where every kill grants **+2 bonus Attunement**. Maximize kills during this window.

## Use Sacrifice Shrines

- Sacrifice Shrines spawn portals for **enemy ambushes** at your location.
- Without shrine-buffing statues, they simply summon extra waves.
- With **Enhanced Shrines** selected, they may additionally grant movement speed, AoE pulses, or chain effects.
- Best used **after completing a Pillar Ritual** — the kill buff + ambush density = maximum Attunement per second.

## Strongroom Boss & Rewards

- Once time runs out (or enough Attunement is gathered), **a boss spawns** — defeat it to complete the Strongroom.
- Boss types:
  - **Standard:** Pulled from the regular dungeon boss pool (Blood Bishop, Tomb Lord, etc.)
  - **Special:** Triggered by Corrupted statues or very high Attunement — can include The Butcher or exclusive seasonal bosses
  - **Modifier-Enhanced:** Inherit modifiers from your earlier statue selections
- After the kill, open the **Grand Horadric Cache**.
- **Higher Attunement = better loot and more materials.**

### Grand Horadric Cache loot includes:
- **Obducite** (Masterworking material)
- **Escalation Sigils** (for Escalating Nightmare chains)
- Gear, gold, crafting materials (quantity/quality scales with Attunement)

### Post-Strongroom Buff
When you leave and return to your Nightmare Dungeon, you receive a **buff for the remainder of the run**. Possible buffs:

| Buff | Effect |
|---|---|
| Experience | Increased XP from monster kills |
| Reputation | Increased reputation from monster kills |
| Obducite | Increased Obducite from monster kills |
| Gold | +25% Gold Find |

## Escalating Nightmares — Strongroom Synergy

- In **Escalating Nightmare Dungeon** chains, subsequent dungeons can spawn **two additional Horadric Portals** leading to extra Strongrooms.
- The Escalation chain (3 dungeons → Astaroth boss fight) is one of the best loops for continuous Sigil + Strongroom farming.
- On **Torment 12**, defeating Astaroth at the end of an Escalation can drop **10,000 Obducite**.

> [!tip] **Best Strongroom loop:** Run Escalating Nightmares on the highest Torment tier you can clear quickly → chain Strongrooms for Attunement → Astaroth for the Obducite jackpot.

## Season 13 Objective: Horadric Secrets

Reaching **Attunement III** inside a Horadric Strongroom on **Torment IV or higher** is a Season 13 challenge objective — rewards a **Mystery Greater Archetype Cache**.

---

## Key Tips

- **Never skip a guaranteed Strongroom sigil** — even with bad negatives, the Cache rewards are strong enough to compensate.
- **Prioritize Pillar Ritual completions** to chain the +2 Attunement windows.
- **Sacrifice Shrines are best used post-ritual** — the ambush density helps hit high Attunement fast.
- **Deaths are punished** — bring enough defense for your tier; a one-shot early costs you the Attunement window.
- **Always lean Corrupted if survivable** — the reward delta between Pure and Corrupted statues is meaningful.
- Strongrooms are one of the primary sources of **Escalation Sigils**, which feed the best endgame farming loop.
- The **5 statues offered are semi-random** — if the pool is weak on a given run, prioritize whatever gives the most kills or mob density.
