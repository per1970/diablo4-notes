# Season 13 Farming Methods — Gems, Gold, XP

> Season 13 is designed around chaining overlapping reward systems. Single-activity farming is the wrong approach — every loop below is more efficient when stacked with at least one other.

---

## 💎 Gem Farming

### Gem Tier Chain
Crude → Chipped → Gem → Flawless → Royal → Grand → Horadric → Flawless Horadric

- Horadric Cube uses a **5-to-1 ratio**: 5 Grands = 1 Horadric, 5 Horadric = 1 Flawless Horadric
- A single Flawless Horadric requires **250 Royal gems** total

### Why Gems Matter More in S13
Gems socketed into **weapons** now provide **multiplicative** damage bonuses (not additive). This makes them one of the strongest character upgrades available:
- **Flawless Horadric** = 32% multiplicative weapon damage
- **Horadric** = 28% multiplicative weapon damage

Match gem type to damage type: Sapphire (cold), Topaz (lightning), Skull (physical). Diamond for all-stats flexibility.

### #1 Farm — Seer's Reach, Skovos *(Expansion required)*
**Location:** Skovos Isles, just north of Temenos

- Run straight to the scorpion boss at the end — skip all side objectives
- Boss drops Royal gems reliably on any Torment tier
  - **Higher Torment:** 3 per kill
  - **Torment 1:** at least 1 per kill, but much faster clears
- Roughly 1 in 17–20 runs yields a direct Grand drop
- Boss drops mixed gem colors — trade extras with clanmates or exchange at the Jeweler rather than brute-forcing every color

### #2 Fallback — Gem Fragment Crafting
- Royal gem = 100,000 gem fragments
- Grand gem = 1,000,000 gem fragments (10 Royal equivalents)
- Slower than Seer's Reach but viable if expansion access is limited

### Socketing Priority
1. Weapons first (multiplicative bonus applies here)
2. For multi-weapon classes, plan the full set before socketing
3. Armor and jewelry fill stat/resistance gaps that Paragon boards don't cover
4. Treat surplus off-color gems as trade currency — don't waste time converting them

## 💰 Gold Farming

Gold sinks in S13 are severe: Masterworking, Occultist rerolls, Paragon resets (tens of millions at 200+), Horadric Cube recipes, and charm crafting all drain fast.

**Universal rule:** Gold bonuses cap at Torment 10. T11 and T12 add nothing to income. Drop to T10 for all gold farming.

---

### #1 — The 900% Gold Seal Trick *(likely unintended — patch risk)*

This is widely considered a Blizzard oversight. The interaction exploits a mismatch between a low-level campaign charm set and how Horadric Seals scale their set bonuses at endgame.

**Step 1 — Acquire Practiced Technique Set (3-piece)**
- Rewarded automatically during the Lord of Hatred main campaign
- You need at minimum the **2-piece bonus** equipped for the trick to activate
- The 3-piece adds another 25% gold find but costs a Talisman slot
- If you salvaged it: you'll need to run the campaign again on a new character

**Step 2 — Roll a Gold Drop Seal via Horadric Cube**
- Endgame Horadric Seals almost always roll class-specific set bonuses — not useful here
- **Workaround:** collect 3 Legendary Horadric Seals → use the **Amalgamate recipe** in the Horadric Cube → the output seal rolls *generic* set bonuses instead
- Keep recycling until you land the Practiced Technique set bonus on the seal
- **Target roll:** 911%–966% increase to gold drop rate
- **Best source for Legendary Seals:** Lair Bosses — funnel drops directly into the Cube

**Step 3 — Stack Torment 10**
- T10 grants a baseline 300% gold drop rate bonus (hard cap — T11/T12 add nothing)
- Combined with the seal roll + Practiced Technique set: ~**1,200% total gold drop rate increase**

**Step 4 — Run Gold Reserve NMDs**
- Slot the **Gold Reserve affix** onto your Nightmare Dungeon Sigils
- Clear the dungeon, open every chest
- At T10 with the full seal setup: ~**20 million gold per chest**
- Multiple chests spawn per dungeon — income stacks fast

> ⚠️ This trick has received significant community and press attention (PC Gamer, Wudijo). A hotfix is plausible. Verify it's still active before investing time in the setup.

---

### #2 — Horadric Strongrooms (~263M gold/hr ceiling)

Horadric Strongrooms are special rooms that spawn inside Nightmare Dungeons.

- **Loop:** run NMD with a Strongroom → clear → sell loot → repeat
- Prioritise Sigils with a **gold find affix** regardless of dungeon layout
- **Mythic Prankster affix** on Sigils can push per-run gold higher with expensive item drops
- Ceiling only reached with fast kill speed — invest in damage and movement speed first

---

### #3 — Kurast Undercity (50M–100M+ per run)

Built around farming and selling **Horandri Seals** to vendors (13M gold each).

- Standard run: 3–5 Seals = **50M+ gold**
- Lucky run (Prankster Goblins): 8–10 Seals = **100M+ gold**
- Skilled players complete a run in ~2.5 minutes

**To maximise Seal output:**
1. Use a **Greater Talisman key** before entering — significantly increases Seal drop rate
2. Activate the **Jade Epiphany node** in your War Plan — each Attunement Rank gained drops XP Globes, each Globe increases the gold and Seal count at final settlement
3. Clear **every brazier on both floors** — each has a 10–15% chance to spawn a Prankster Goblin, which doubles the final reward (two Goblins = triple Seal count)
4. Kill enough enemies for 3–4 Attunement Ranks, then focus the boss — you do not need to clear everything

> Also generates ~12M bonus XP per run from Attunement Rank Globes — you're pushing Paragon at the same time.

---

### #4 — Infernal Hordes (~110M gold/hr)

- Unlock via the one-time quest "The Eyes of the Enemy"
- Infernal Compasses drop from Helltide Chests, Whisper Caches, NMDs, and the Pit
- At end of run: always choose **Spoils of Gold** to convert leftover Burning Aether directly to gold
- Also generates crafting materials and gear — better than Strongrooms if you want loot alongside gold

---

### #5 — Helltide + Tree of Whispers (passive layering)

- Runs continuously — no downtime between sessions
- Kill elites, open Tortured Caches, activate every Greed Shrine
- Overlap Whisper objectives in the same zone — no extra time cost
- Use [helltides.com](https://helltides.com) to track active zones and secret chest locations
- War Plan node **Tree of Plenty** adds a fourth Whisper Cache per turn-in
- Clear inventory before re-entering — a full bag means missed drops

## ⚡ XP / Paragon Farming

> S13 intentionally slowed Paragon XP. S12's Paragon 300 was considered a mistake — expect a more deliberate grind. XP gains are tuned down; efficiency and system-chaining matter far more than raw playtime.

**Key rule:** Torment 12 has a +1400% kill XP bonus. That number is a trap. T10 vs T12 is not a meaningful quality difference — reward improvements largely plateau after T10. Farm T10 at speed, not T12 at a crawl.

---

### #1 — Kurast Undercity + Jade Epiphany *(Best Paragon XP/hr)*

- Optimised runs on T10–T12 yield **20–30 million XP per run**
- Attunement levelling spawns XP orbs as you climb ranks — faster clears = more orbs
- Hit every beacon even after attunement is maxed: Prankster Goblins can spawn and juice the end-of-run tribute chest
- Players chaining this efficiently have hit Paragon 200+ within the first days of the season

---

### #2 — Helltide + War Plans Chain *(Most Accessible / Best Levelling Loop)*

- Chain War Plans with Helltide as the core activity
- Prioritise the **middle path in the Helltide Activity Skill Tree** — boosts Hellwyrm spawns and maggot explosions that drop large XP globes
- **Loop:** farm cinders + complete War Plan objectives → kill Blood Maiden → pick up Whisper Caches → turn in completed War Plan at Temis for XP burst → repeat
- Add Legion Events for bonus Boss Trophies (5 of the same trophy → Horadric Cube Amalgamation → random Unique from that boss's loot table)
- Worm events and world spawns (enemies bursting from the ground) drop XP orbs — chain these when available

---

### #3 — The Pit *(Late Paragon Push / Best Raw XP Ceiling)*

- Unlock: complete Pit Tier 10 to open Torment 1
- **Speed is everything:** a 1.5-minute Pit 100 clear = same XP/min as a 2-minute Pit 145 clear
- Farm the highest tier where you're limited only by movement speed, not kill speed
- Two-minute clears at Tier 40 beat ten-minute clears at Tier 60 every time
- War Plan node **Heart of Stone** makes monsters drop XP progression orbs — especially effective on dense packs (rat kings, spider clusters)
- Also the primary activity for **Glyph upgrades** — Haste nodes stack upgrade chances on top of XP returns

---

### #4 — War Plans *(Multiplier for All Other Activities)*

War Plans are not a farm activity — they're the system that makes every other farm stronger.

- Each completed step: minimum **100,000 XP**
- Submitting a fully completed plan: bonus rewards on top
- Completing activities earns Activity XP → unlocks skill tree upgrades per activity (up to 7 points per tree)
- You get **3 rerolls** per new plan — refresh until you get Helltide-heavy or Undercity-heavy objectives
- **Jade Epiphany node** (1 point): XP orbs on every Attunement Rank gained in Undercity — essential

> If your War Plans tree is not optimised, every other XP farm is running below its potential.

---

### Alt Levelling — Whisper Cache Banking

- Stockpile Whisper Caches on a high-level main character
- Open them on a fresh alt character at the highest possible Torment tier
- Caches scale with the Torment tier at which you **open** them, not where you earned the Grim Favors
- Most efficient alt levelling method currently available

## 🔄 Optimal Daily Loop (Endgame)

| Order | Activity | Primary Gain | Secondary Gain |
|-------|----------|-------------|----------------|
| 1 | Kurast Undercity (Jade Epiphany active) | Paragon XP | Horandri Seals → gold |
| 2 | Horadric Strongroom NMDs (Gold Reserve sigil) | Gold | War Plans progress |
| 3 | Helltide overlapping Whispers | XP + loot | Gold + War Plans progress |
| 4 | Sell haul in Temis | Gold | — |
| 5 | Turn in completed War Plan at Command Table | XP burst + rewards | Activity tree upgrades |

Repeat. The systems are designed to chain — running them in isolation wastes half their value.
