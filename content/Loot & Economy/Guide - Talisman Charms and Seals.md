# Guide – Talisman: Charms & Seals

> Sources: wudijo (YouTube, May 2 2026) · ggwtb.com · boostmatch.gg · Fextralife Wiki · Blizzard Watch

---

## Overview

The Talisman is a permanent Lord of Hatred system with its own inventory tab — 1 central Seal slot + up to 6 outer Charm slots. It is build-defining at Torment difficulty and does not compete with gear slots. Unlocked by completing the *Last of the Horadrim* questline during the LoH campaign.

> ⚠️ Known bug: In party play, the Talisman tab sometimes never appears. Requires relog or party reset.

---

## Seals

The Seal goes in the central slot and determines how many Charm slots you have. Without a Seal, no Charms can be equipped.

**Rarity → Charm Slots:**
- Common: ~3 slots
- Legendary: 5 slots base (can roll +1 = 6 slots)
- Mythic: 6 slots + special bonuses

**Chase target: Legendary Seal with +1 Charm Slot (6 total)**

The +1 Charm Slot is the only way to reach 6 slots on a Legendary Seal. It cannot be crafted directly — you must either find it or cube 3 Seals together for a chance at it. Drop rate is roughly **1-in-50** via the Horadric Cube 3-to-1 recipe. This is the primary bottleneck.

**Seal Affix Priority:**
1. +1 Charm Slot (mandatory for 6-slot)
2. Large damage multiplier (e.g. 16%+ damage multi)
3. Set bonus affix that matches your Charm set
4. Life / All Stats as filler

Seals can have powerful affixes but **cannot be freely rerolled** — only the 3-to-1 cube recipe can change what you get.

## Set Charms

Set Charms drop from Torment I onward. There are 5 set piece types per class set. If you have any piece from your desired set, the Horadric Cube's **Roll Set Charm** recipe converts it into another random piece from the same set — this is the primary way to complete a set without relying on drops.

Rolling can also produce Ancestral-quality Set Charms, including Greater Affixes.

**Target Stats per Charm:**
- **Primary:** +3 Skill Ranks (to your main skill category — e.g. Occult, Hellfire)
- **Secondary:** All Stats

**Ideal 5-piece setup:** 4× (+3 Skill Ranks) + 2× (All Stats)

With a full 5-piece set at +3 ranks each, you gain +15 skill ranks from Charms alone — effectively doubling your skill ranks from gear investment. All Stats is lower raw damage but activates Paragon nodes that require stat thresholds — especially relevant now that Shroud of False Death is no longer universal.

**Other situational stats:** Resources (Barbarian), Movement Speed (if not capped).

## Unique Charms

Unique Charms carry the power of a Unique item without occupying a gear slot. You cannot double-dip — equipping a Unique Charm while wearing the same Unique item is not allowed.

**Crafting Recipe (Horadric Cube):**
- 1× Ancestral version of the target Unique
- 3× any other Unique items (can be junk)
- Result: Unique Charm with randomized power roll

**Important caveats:**
- Do **not** reroll the Unique Power before converting — the power value is re-randomized during conversion anyway
- Two-handed weapon values are halved when converted to a Charm (same mechanic as aspects)
- Amulet bonus is removed on conversion
- The Unique Power **cannot be rerolled** after the Charm is created — the roll you get is final
- Non-ancestral Unique items can still drop as random Unique Charms from the world loot pool

**Top craft candidates (general):** Banish Lord's Talisman, Red Blessing, class key-passive amulets (especially Spiritborn/Warlock/Paladin).

## Mythic Seals

Mythic Seals are the rarest Talisman items in the game. The best Mythic Seal allows you to hold a **full 5-piece set bonus + a 3-piece set bonus simultaneously** — a massive power spike for finished builds. There are 3 Mythic Seals total; the other two are considered too weak for most builds currently.

Mythic Seals also roll random affixes like Legendary Seals.

**Sources:**
- **Uber Mephisto** (Crux of the False Prophet key required) — the only boss with a confirmed Mythic Seal drop entry on his loot table
- **Echoing Hatred** — can drop at deep waves (wudijo confirmed one personal drop here)
- **Undercity Tributes** with Charm reward modifiers
- **War Plans** activities with extra Charm rewards
- **Season Rank rewards** — at least one Mythic Horadric Seal is a confirmed Season Rank track reward

**Rarity context:** wudijo found 1 Mythic Seal across ~20+ Mythic Unique drops. Treat it as a long-term chase item, not a gearing target. Build around a 6-slot Legendary Seal first.

> 💡 Mephisto farming note: Drop is not guaranteed per kill. Save Crux of the False Prophet keys for when your group can kill cleanly — failed attempts waste the key.

## Gold Income: Rendering Extras

Once your Talisman is set, surplus Seals and Charms have strong gold value at the **Purveyor of Curiosities** (render, not salvage):

- Legendary Seal: ~13.3 million gold each
- Set Charm (small): ~1.5 million gold each

With enchanting costs being very high in Season 13, rendering surplus Talisman pieces is a meaningful gold income source.

- [ ] Complete LoH campaign → unlock Talisman tab

- [ ] Identify your class set and target pieces
- [ ] Use Cube's Roll Set Charm to complete your set
- [ ] Chase 4× (+3 Skill Ranks) + 2× (All Stats) on Charms
- [ ] Craft a Unique Charm from an Ancestral Unique (use junk Uniques as catalysts)
- [ ] Render surplus Seals/Charms at Purveyor for gold
- [ ] (Long-term) Farm Uber Mephisto / Echoing Hatred for Mythic Seal
