# Whisper Caches — Chaos vs Mystery

## Collection of Chaos — The Baseline

The standard "wildcard" cache from the Tree of Whispers.

- Most cache offerings tell you which item type they contain (Collection of Gauntlets, Collection of Two-Handed Weapons, etc.)
- **Collection of Chaos is different** — it provides high-end rewards but the **gear type is randomized** rather than tied to a specific slot
- Helltides now spawn 10 Grim Favors' worth of Whispers, so you can get a full cache each Helltide
- Chance to be offered a **Greater Collection** instead — contains more items with at least one Legendary or higher

> [!info] Why Chaos is good
> Random gear slot, but generally one of the better picks because you trade slot specificity for higher overall yield. **Greater Chaos** historically has the highest gold/loot density of any cache type.

## Collection of Mystery — The S13 War Plan Upgrade

**Mystery caches don't appear naturally.** They only show up if you have a specific **War Plans perk** active.

### The Perk: Grim Mysteries

> [!star] Grim Mysteries
> **Collection of Chaos are replaced with Collection of Mysteries.**

This is a perk in the **Tree of Whispers** branch of the War Plans Activity Skill Tree.

When you've unlocked it:
- Every time the Tree would offer you a Collection of Chaos, it offers a **Collection of Mystery instead**
- This is a **strict upgrade** — same wildcard concept, but with significantly higher tier rewards

## Community Pick Order

| Without Grim Mysteries | With Grim Mysteries |
|---|---|
| Greater Collection of Chaos | Collection of Mystery / Greater Collection of Mystery |

## Practical Implication

If you're running War Plans regularly (which you should be in S13):

> [!tip] Priority unlock
> Prioritize unlocking **Grim Mysteries** in the Tree of Whispers Activity Skill Tree early.

Activity Perks unlocked from War Plans **affect every endgame activity** regardless of whether they're in a War Plan or not — so even when you're casually farming Whispers outside of a War Plan run, your Chaos caches will be **auto-upgraded to Mystery caches**.

## Complementary Perk: Fortune or Famine

Same tree, worth pairing:

> Whisper Caches from the Tree of Whispers are always at least of **Greater Quality or Junk**.

Pair Grim Mysteries + Fortune or Famine and your Whisper farming becomes meaningfully more rewarding per turn-in.

## TL;DR

| Cache | What it is |
|---|---|
| **Chaos** | Base random-slot cache, available to everyone |
| **Mystery** | Upgraded version replacing Chaos once you've unlocked **Grim Mysteries** in the War Plans Tree of Whispers Activity Skill Tree |

Same wildcard concept, better rewards. **If you see Mystery as an option, take it.**
