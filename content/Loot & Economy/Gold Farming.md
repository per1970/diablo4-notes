# Gold Farming — Season 13

## TL;DR

**Infernal Hordes for raw gold-per-hour, Helltides for everyone else.** Critically: **stop salvaging gear if gold is your bottleneck**.

## The Single Biggest Mindset Shift

> [!warning] Stop reflexively salvaging
> One of the biggest mistakes in Diablo 4 Season 13 is salvaging too much gear. **If your current bottleneck is gold, you should not be dismantling every unwanted item by default.**

In many cases, selling extra Rares, Sacreds, and Legendaries is the difference between staying poor and building a healthy gold reserve.

### The Simple Rule

| Need | Action |
|---|---|
| Gold | **Sell** |
| Materials | Salvage |
| High market value | Trade |

People salvage on autopilot then complain about being broke. Set your loot filter to keep yellows visible, sell the ones you don't want as Cube bases, and you'll comfortably double passive gold income without changing what you do.

## Tier 1: Infernal Hordes (Best Ceiling)

**Best direct gold farm for geared endgame players.**

- Each successful run dumps a massive pile of gear ripe for vendoring
- The **Spoils of Gold** chest is literally a gold cash-out (leftover Aether after Greater Equipment chests)
- High-Torment Hordes give scaled item drops that vendor for huge sums
- Stacks with War Plans Activity Tree perks

> [!info] Build gating
> Requires a build that can clear **T8+ Hordes** efficiently. Below that tier the gold-per-time falls off relative to Helltides.

## Tier 2: Helltides (Best for Most Players)

**Best overall and most accessible gold farming method.**

Why it wins for the majority:

- **No build gating** — works at any Torment tier you can survive
- **Tortured Gifts (cinder chests)** drop dense legendaries → all vendorable
- The **Blood Maiden** event dumps loot every 60–90 seconds when juiced
- Farm cinders, materials, and gold simultaneously
- Easy to combine with Whispers (most Helltides have Grim Favors active)

This is the lowest-friction option in the game right now.

## Tier 3: Tree of Whispers (Best Passive Layer)

**Run on top of other content, not as a dedicated farm.**

- Cache turn-ins give big gold lump sums
- Helltides spawn full 10-Grim-Favor's worth of Whispers anyway → free cache every Helltide
- **Greater Collection of Chaos** (or Mystery if you've unlocked Grim Mysteries) is the gold-densest cache choice

See: [[Whisper Caches - Chaos vs Mystery]]

## Tier 4: Kurast Undercity (Sleeper Pick)

For gold *plus* legendary density to sell. **Tribute-controlled output** means you can stack gold-focused Tributes to maximize per-run yield.

## The S13 Multiplier — War Plans

> [!important] Never run activities outside of an active War Plan
> The single most important concept in Diablo 4 Lord of Hatred endgame farming is that you **never do an activity outside of your active War Plan**. Every run outside your War Plan is bonus loot and Activity Tree progress you threw into a fire.

### Gold-Focused Activity Tree Nodes

**Helltide tree**
- Increased Tortured Gift density
- Demonic rat packs that drop bonus cinders/loot
- **Hell's Price** (a 666-cinder chest) — particularly juicy

**Lair Boss tree**
- Nodes that enhance gold and Rune rewards from boss kills

**Tree of Whispers tree**
- **Grim Mysteries** (Chaos → Mystery cache upgrade)
- **Fortune or Famine** (caches always Greater Quality or Junk)

## The Optimal Gold Loop

1. **Build a War Plan** with two Helltides, one Whispers, one Lair Boss, one filler
2. **Run it at the highest Torment your build can clear deathlessly**
   - Deaths in Helltide cost cinders — big efficiency loss
3. **Sell everything yellow and orange** unless you're specifically short on materials
4. **Cash in Whisper caches as Greater Collection of Chaos/Mystery** for big gold lumps
5. **Re-run with rotating War Plan** — same activities, fresh node rewards
6. **Once geared enough, swap to Infernal Hordes-heavy plans** for the higher gold ceiling

## Veteran Trick Most Don't Use

> [!tip] Burn leftover Aether on Spoils of Gold
> If you finish a Hordes run with 380 Aether (just shy of the 400 needed for Greater Equipment), don't let it sit there.

Convert the lot into **Spoils of Gold** and **Spoils of Materials**.

- **Aether evaporates between runs**
- **Gold doesn't**

People throw away thousands of gold a session by leaving sub-400 Aether totals unspent.

## Universal S13 Money Grind Summary

**Helltides + War Plans + Whispers** is the universal-tier money grind. Push to **Infernal Hordes at high Torment** once your build supports it. **Stop salvaging** gear when you need gold. Always run inside an active War Plan with the **Helltide and Whispers Activity Trees** being your priority for gold-focused perks.
