# Diablo 4 – Season 13: Season of Reckoning
**Expansion:** Lord of Hatred (Patch 3.0.0)
**Launch Date:** April 27–28, 2026 (alongside Season 13 reset)
**Season Duration:** ~90 days (est. end ~July 2026)

---

## 🆕 NEW CLASSES (Permanent – Lord of Hatred)
- **Paladin** – Returns from D2. Holy/aura-based. Blessed Hammer, Condemn, Aura skills. Angelic Form ultimate. Sword-and-board playstyle. Early access for pre-order; nerfed slightly at S13 launch.
- **Warlock** – Brand new to the franchise. Demon binder with two archetypes: **Legion** (swarm summoning) and **Vanguard** (melee hellfire). Dual-resource system, Infernal Transformation mechanic. Complex skill interactions, minimal built-in defense – must itemize for survivability.

---

## 🗺️ NEW REGION (Permanent – Lord of Hatred)
- **Skovos Isles** – New explorable zone. Ancient Amazon homeland, volcanic coasts, dense forests, waterlogged ruins. Endgame hub city: **Temis**.

---

## ⚙️ NEW PERMANENT SYSTEMS (Lord of Hatred)

### Horadric Cube (Crafting)
- Major new crafting system unlocked during the LoH campaign.
- Can: add/remove/reroll affixes, reroll items into new random items of same quality, upgrade all item types, craft Unique Charms, create upgraded gems (beyond Jeweler), reroll Set Charms, transmute items, craft Legendary Runes for Runewords and Mythic Uniques.
- Requires **3 copies of the same Unique** to reroll into a new version – farming commitment tripled.

### Talisman System
- New item slot holding multiple **Charms** and **Seals**.
- **Set Charms** grant set bonuses (5-piece sets, scaling 2-piece → higher-tier bonuses). First class set bonuses in D4's history.
- **Unique Charms**: any Unique can be converted – the Unique power carries over, but attributes/affixes do not. Frees up gear slots. Build-defining Uniques no longer lock item slots.
- Key craft candidates: Banish Lord's Talisman, Red Blessing, Spiritborn key-passive amulets.

### War Plans (Endgame)
- Unlocked after completing the LoH campaign in Skovos.
- Acts like an endgame playlist/scheduler for all activities (NMDs, Whispers, Helltide, Infernal Hordes, Pit, Undercity, Lair Bosses, etc.).
- Each activity has its own skill tree and progression – customizable modifiers (e.g., summon bosses mid-dungeon, choose loot type).
- Foundation for future seasonal updates.

### Echoing Hatred
- Endless wave survival mode. Triggered via rare **Trace of Echoes** item.
- Enemies spawn continuously, scaling in difficulty until player dies.
- Ultimate build stress test. Deep waves drop exclusive loot. Builds typically hit a wall at wave 20–30 if not optimized.

### Loot Filter (Native)
- Found in Options > Gameplay.
- Can hide, show, or color-code gear drops, inventory items, stash items, and vendor gear.
- Does NOT affect non-gear items (Temper manuals, reagents, gems, currency).
- Up to 25 rules per filter.

### Skill Tree Rework (All 8 Classes)
- Each active skill now has **3 branches**: first two give general upgrade effects, third unlocks **3 Skill Variants**.
- Skill Variants can change a skill's elemental/mechanical tags entirely (e.g., frost-converted Hydra no longer benefits from fire damage nodes or affixes).
- Two of three variants available to all players; full variant access requires LoH expansion.
- Critical for Paragon synergy – skill tag changes cascade into board interactions.

### Fishing
- New casual activity available at any beach or large body of water.

## 📊 CORE SYSTEM CHANGES (All Players)

### Level Cap & Torment
- **Level cap raised from 60 → 70**.
- **Torment tiers expanded from T4 → T12** (more gradual difficulty scaling).

### Itemization
- **Item Power cap raised**: 850 (non-ancestral), 900 (ancestral).
- **Unique affixes now roll randomly** – fixed secondary affixes are gone. Same Unique can roll 3 different affix combos.
- **Tempering now works on Uniques and Mythic items**.
- Damage multipliers added to gear; can stack with themselves.
- Reduced fixed affixes on Uniques – more affix pool variety.

### Overpower Rework
- No longer a random pseudo-crit. Now a **stackable buff (up to 4 stacks by default**, extendable via items/aspects).
- Consumed deliberately by specific skills.
- Class-specific ceilings: Sorceress can reach 12 stacks via stacked max Overpower + Star Coordinate.
- Necromancer: Blood Orb economy (orbs → stacks → damage multiplier).

### Paragon
- Glyph upgrades gated behind Artificer's Tier thresholds.
- Glyph level to turn Legendary changed from 25 to 50
- Max level for Glyph : 150
- Raw stat nodes largely removed from boards; boards now exist to serve Glyph bonuses, Legendary nodes, and skill-tag synergies.
- Paragon Points also awarded via Season Rank track.

## 📅 SEASON 13 SPECIFIC (Seasonal – Temporary)

### Season Name: Season of Reckoning
- **⚠️ No unique seasonal mechanic or seasonal questline** – stripped back due to expansion resources.
- No seasonal reputation system.
- No seasonal-exclusive items in loot pool.

### Season Rank
- **9 Ranks** of objectives (100+ total challenges).
- Rewards: Resplendent Sparks, Caches, Crafting Materials, Paragon Points, gear caches.
- Some objectives require LoH expansion (Skovos access gated).

### Smoldering Ashes / Season Blessings (Urns)
- Urn of Curiosities – extra item chance from Purveyor of Curiosities
- Urn of Reclamation – increased rare materials from salvage
- Urn of Masterworking – extra Obducite drop chance
- Urn of Glyphs – chance for extra Glyph upgrade
- Urn of Ancestral Whispers – chance for Ancestral Cache on Whisper turn-in

### Battle Pass
- Free + Premium Reliquaries.
- Farm in-game Tokens for weapon cosmetics.
- Premium/Deluxe for exclusive Armor Set cosmetics.

## ❌ REMOVED / GONE FROM SEASON 12

- **Killstreaks** (Season 12 mechanic) – removed at reset.
- **Bloodied Sigils** (Season 12 mechanic) – removed at reset.
- All **Season 12 seasonal reputation** and related rewards gone.
- **DOOM: The Dark Ages collaboration** cosmetics (Season 12 collab) – no longer earnable.
- **Fixed secondary affixes on Uniques** – replaced with random rolls.
- **Raw stat additive damage nodes** on most Paragon boards – largely removed.
- **Torment 4 as difficulty ceiling** – replaced by T12 system.
- **Level 60 cap** – now 70.
- Pit portal spawn after completing a run (hotfix removed post-launch).
- Removed Bloodied Horde / Butcher mechanic
- Incenses and Elixirs removed

## 🏛️ CONTEXT NOTES (Build Crafting)
- Season 13 is intentionally light on seasonal content – LoH expansion carries the weight.
- Precedent: similar to Season 6 + Vessel of Hatred launch.
- Spiritborn: dominant in S12, nerfed for S13 but still strong.
- Paladin: extremely strong at LoH launch despite nerfs.
- Warlock: high complexity, high ceiling, fragile – requires careful itemization for defense.
- The Pit is now the central endgame loop (not optional).
- Horadric Cube + Talisman are the most impactful new build systems for theorycrafting.
