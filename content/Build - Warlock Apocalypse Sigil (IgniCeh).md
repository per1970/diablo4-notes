## Build Analysis

### Core Damage Loop
1. **Build Overpower stacks** — Banished Lord's Talisman generates 4 stacks per 275 resource spent. Ring of Starless Skies makes resource spending also reduce skill costs and ramps damage up to 50%[x]. With stacking to 12, that's 93%[+] × 12 = over 1,100%[+] multiplicative Overpower bonus sitting on every hit.
2. **Stack kills in Sigil of Chaos** — Fleshwrit Carapace passively banks 1 kill/second on active Sigils, and unused kills carry over on expiry. This pre-loads Sigil of Chaos with a kill count before Apocalypse is cast.
3. **Cast Apocalypse inside Sigil of Chaos** — Hands of the Worldbreaker converts Apocalypse into a Sigil Skill and scales its damage up to 400%[x] based on stored kills. With Fleshwrit's 90%[x] Sigil Skill damage bonus on top, a fully loaded Apocalypse hits for an enormous burst.
4. **Rite of the Nameless Ritual** — The 5-set means a single Sigil cast spawns all base Sigils simultaneously, instantly progressing the Ritual and providing 200%[x] increased damage to enemies standing in them. The Ritual itself ramps from 100%[x] to 200%[x] as it approaches its 60-second end, then releases a free Occult window.
5. **Fire multipliers** — The Grandfather contributes x105% Fire Damage Multiplier twice (rolled twice). Cremator's Perplex Round adds another 100%[x] when at 4+ Overpower stacks. These multiply off each other for massive fire damage.

### Key Synergies
- **Heir of Perdition** extends Sigil Duration by 75%, meaning kills accumulate longer in each Sigil of Chaos before Apocalypse is cast.
- **The Hemat Stone** doubles Command Valloch and Ritualist Fragment potency and boosts Occult Skill damage by 30%[x] — directly amplifying the Ritual's free-cast window.
- **Scepter of the Three** lets all Ultimates be slotted for ranks and builds up to 100%[x] bonus damage while they're inactive, which gets dumped on the next activation.
- **Seal of the Diamond Mind** reduces the Rite of the Nameless requirement by 1, meaning the 2-set Ritual bonus activates with only 1 charm — but here all 5 are equipped for the full 5-set.
- **Footfalls of the Waning World** enables Nether Step as the evade with Shadowform stacks, giving both mobility and Stealth for repositioning in the Pit.
- **CirPrid gem** on pants auto-casts Dark Prison via the 5-skill proc, providing free CC tethering without burning a skill slot.

### Overpower Math (Summary)
- Base cap: 4 stacks (default)
- Extended to 12 via items/aspects
- Each stack: 93%[+] damage increase
- 12 stacks = ~1,116%[+] Overpower bonus
- Banished Lord's Talisman: crits deal +12%[x] per stack (12 × 12% = +144%[x] on crits)
- Cremator's Perplex Round: 4+ stacks → Fire Skills +100%[x]
- Gloves: +15.9% damage per Overpower Stack
- Ring 1: +16.1% damage per Overpower Stack
- Ring 2: +15.0% damage per Overpower Stack
- Amulet: +15.3% damage per Overpower Stack

### Survivability
This is a classic high-ceiling/low-floor Warlock setup — it hits extremely hard but defense requires active management:
- Damage Reduction from 2H Imprint on pants: 40%
- Rite of the Nameless (3) Set: 20% DR ramping during Ritual
- Life pool is substantial: 8,206 (Grandfather) + ~10,000+ from other gear
- Hemat Stone provides in-combat healing (2.8% Max Life per skill cast, doubled while Injured)
- Dodge: 9.7% Chance + 4 Evade charges (Footfalls), converted to Nether Step
- The build is heavily susceptible to burst if Shadowform stacks are depleted and Ritual isn't active — the lack of inherent Warlock defense means repositioning discipline is mandatory

### Strengths
- Enormous single-cast burst via Apocalypse in a loaded Sigil of Chaos
- Fully maxed Paragon glyphs (all Level 150) — no further optimization ceiling there
- Mythic Unique in 3 slots (Heir of Perdition, The Grandfather, Ring of Starless Skies) — near-BiS gear foundation
- Ritual engine provides sustained damage ramp with free Occult cast window on completion
- 5-set Rite of the Nameless is one of the strongest Talisman setups available for Warlock

### Weaknesses / Watch Points
- Kill accumulation in Sigil of Chaos is time-gated — against single targets (bosses) the kill bank fills slowly
- The Sigil of Subversion + Bombardment rotation needs to be clean to keep the Ritual progressing
- Heir of Perdition's Mother's Favor mechanic requires slaughtering enemies to maintain — drops off in sparse content
- Fragile without the Ritual's 20% DR active; getting caught mid-rotation in the Pit is punishing
