# Fortify Mechanics — Warlock Focus

## How Fortify Works

Fortify is essentially a **second life pool overlaid on your health bar**. Characters take **10% decreased damage when Fortified** — meaning when you have **more Fortify than current Life**. Fortify is tracked on your life globe as an extra layer of red.

### Key mechanical details

- **Trigger condition:** You're "Fortified" (gaining the 10% DR) only when **current Life ≤ Fortify value**
  - At full HP with low Fortify, you're **not** Fortified yet
  - You have to take some damage first — this is the counter-intuitive part many players miss
- **Decay:** After 5 seconds out of combat, Fortify rapidly depletes unless you gain more (each new Fortify gain delays decay another 5 seconds)
- **Healing doesn't restore it:** You don't regain Fortify when you heal. **Chugging a potion can drop you out of Fortified** — this is the trap
- **Boosts Overpower:** Overpower damage formula uses current Life + current Fortify, so Fortify-heavy builds also indirectly buff their burst
- **Sapphires scale it:** Sapphires socketed into armor increase your Damage Reduction while Fortified — stack them if Fortified is part of your defensive plan

## ⚠️ S13 Change: Warlock Now Fortify-Eligible

Historically, Fortify could only be utilized by **Barbarian, Druid, and Necromancer**.

**Older guides will tell you Warlock can't get it natively — that's outdated.** With Lord of Hatred's launch, Warlock has been given Fortify access through skills, upgrades, and Aspects.

## Warlock-Specific Fortify Sources in S13

### Skill-based triggers

**Rampage (Brute summon) with the Demon Masher upgrade**
- Rampage smashes the Warlock's own lesser demons to grant them Fortify
- **Cleanest and most reliable source for summoner builds**
- Keep lesser demons up (Fallen Lunatics from Command Fallen, Sigil of Summons demons), then let your Brute pulp them

### Legendary Aspects

**Gorefeast**
- *If you have Eviscerated an enemy in the last 10s you gain [30-50]% increased Fortify and it refills your Life 200% faster*
- Boosts Fortify generation rather than triggering it directly
- Pairs with any other source

**Cauterization**
- *Taking Direct Damage while you have Volatility has a 75% chance to either strike you or an additional 3% of your Maximum Life as Fire damage or Fortify you for [3.00-6.00]% of your Maximum Life*
- Reactive trigger gated behind having Volatility active
- Solid for Hellfire builds

### Generic sources that always work

- **Armor with "Chance when struck to Fortify life" affix** — works on Warlock too
- **Paragon nodes** — several glyph radii and rare nodes grant Fortify-on-X conditions

## Practical Synergies

### Legion Shard Summoner Build

The cleanest Fortify-driven setup:

1. Spam Command Fallen / Sigil of Summons to keep lesser demons stocked
2. Cast Rampage (Demon Masher) — the Brute eats your demons, you get Fortify
3. Stack **Gorefeast** if using a skill that Eviscerates, to boost amount per trigger
4. Socket **Sapphires** in armor to push damage reduction past the base 10%

### Hellfire/Volatility Build

Lean on **Cauterization** + the "when struck" affix + Sapphires. Less consistent than the summoner approach but works as a reactive layer.

## Veteran Tip

Don't try to make Warlock into a Fortify-tank like a Barbarian — the class doesn't have the deep generation kit Barb does.

Treat Fortify on Warlock as a **secondary defensive layer** on top of your real survivability:

- Shadowform stacks
- Demonform uptime
- Barriers from gear

The 10% DR is nice, but the Warlock's defensive identity is still about being **elusive** (Shadowform/Stealth) and **trading life for power**, not soaking hits.
