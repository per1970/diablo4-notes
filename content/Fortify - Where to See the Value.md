# Fortify — Where to See the Value

## Location: The Health Globe (Bottom-Left)

Fortify shows up as a **brighter, lighter red layer** overlaid on your normal (darker) health globe. It builds up from the bottom of the orb upward as you generate it.

## What to Look For

### Two Shades of Red

- **Darker red** = your actual Life
- **Brighter/lighter red** = Fortify, sitting on top of your Life
- The boundary between them is what you're tracking

### The Fortified Ring

When your Fortify value meets or exceeds your current Life — meaning you're actually getting the **10% damage reduction** — an **iron-spiked ring** appears around the health globe.

> [!important] The ring is the real indicator
> - **Ring visible** = Fortified is *active*, you're getting the DR
> - **No ring** = you have Fortify stored, but it isn't doing anything yet

### Numerical Value on Hover

Hover your cursor over the health globe. The tooltip shows:

- Current Life / Max Life
- Current Fortify amount as a number

## The Thing That Confuses Most Players

You can have a full Fortify bar visible on your globe and still **not be Fortified**.

Remember: the 10% DR only applies when **current Life ≤ Fortify value**.

- At full HP with a stack of Fortify, you're not getting the reduction yet
- The spiked ring won't appear until you take a hit and your Life drops to or below the Fortify threshold

The **colored bar tells you how much Fortify is *available*** to start mitigating once you take damage. The **ring tells you whether you're *currently* Fortified**.
